# Model Rules

## Sessions
- Asia
- London
- Premarket: 7:00–9:30 AM New York
- Entry window: 9:30–10:30 AM New York

## Long Setup
1. Sellside liquidity is swept
2. A bearish FVG gets inverted
3. Price retests the inverted FVG
4. Entry triggers on the retest
5. Stop goes below the last swing low
6. Target is the nearest opposing session high

## Short Setup
1. Buyside liquidity is swept
2. A bullish FVG gets inverted
3. Price retests the inverted FVG
4. Entry triggers on the retest
5. Stop goes above the last swing high
6. Target is the nearest opposing session low
